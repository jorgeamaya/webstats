Chapter 1 About

This site compiles online resources in the areas of Experimental Design, Machine Learning, and Bioinformatics. For the moment, it works as a repository of materials I use in my own work and often recommend or discuss with others.

In addition, there are links to scientific papers and outreach materials that I find relevant.
